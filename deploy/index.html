About

Hi, friend! I'm Jeremy.

I am a community organizer in the youth climate movement and #OccupyTogether movement. My passion is building web apps for activists and I spend most of my time developing Rally Room, an online team tool belt for organizers. I also have a day job building Django and Drupal web apps.

I recently graduated from the University of Oregon with a degree in environmental studies and a minor in computer science.
