What is a coach?

A coach is someone who partners with you and supports you in:

  • Shifting your attention away from your worries toward seeing who you really are
  • Discovering what holds meaning and purpose for you
  • Creating goals that come alive with meaning and excitement
  • Bringing your goals into reality with ease, by setting specific goals

A coach is not a counselor or therapist, however, a coach could help you see if seeing a mental health professional would be supportive. A coach cannot provide legal, financial, medical, or consultation services, however, a coach could help you see whether to seek support in one of these areas.

Is coaching for me?

If you can say “yes” to any of these questions, coaching has something to offer you:

  • Do doubt, worry, and fear consume my energy and hold me back from taking action on things that are important to me?
  • Do I regularly feel frustrated with money, time, friends, co-workers, etc.?
  • Do I have a dream for a project, adventure, organization that I haven’t moved on yet?
  • Am I finding more struggle in my life than there needs to be with you money, time, relationships, creativity, hobbies, work, etc? Is this struggle so familiar that I just think it’s fine because you can “just deal with it”?
  • Do I know that I’m not bringing my full authentic self to some parts of my life?
  • Am I wondering what my next big step in life is? Do I have too many options to choose from? Or do I feel like I have no options?

Coaching is a good fit for you if you want to turn the frustrating parts of your life into fulfilling parts, and make the sweet parts of your life even sweeter.

Who is Jeremy?
