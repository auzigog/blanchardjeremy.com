A view into all your teams

When it's your job to support a bunch of teams of changemakers, it can be hard to have a view into their work so you can offer support as needed. RallyRoom gives the teams an effective tool for organizing tasks and meetings, and you instantly get a summary of what they're working on, so you can support them and they can reach out for support from you.

Empower teams to track tasks & meetings

Tasks deserve a good home. A home that's better than a row on a Google Spreadsheet. With RallyRoom, each of your teams' tasks have a place to thrive: they get followed up on, it's easy to know who is doing what, and to communicate about them tasks as they evolve. RallyRoom supports tasks from where they sprout: during meetings. Keep track of meeting reminders, agendas, notes, and task collection.

Let your teams see each other

In an organization with many teams on-the-ground, it's hard to feel connected and to share hard-won lessons to other people doing the same work. Get a view into other teams and have an open communication channel to share your knowledge and learn from other teams.
